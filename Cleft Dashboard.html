Cleft


Courage. Legacy. Endurance. Faithfulness. Trust.

This is not just an acronym for "cleft" but they are what every Cleft member is striving for. These are not the end goal of our walk, but they are the basic fundamentals of our character as disciples of Christ, leaders of our God-given ministries, and as our brothers' keepers.
