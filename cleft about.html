Cleft YM 2025

C – Courage (Resilience: Health, Eating, Fitness)

Definition: Strength and resilience to face challenges boldly. Verse: “Have I not commanded you? Be strong and of good courage; do not be afraid, nor be dismayed, for the Lord your God is with you wherever you go.” — Joshua 1:9 (NKJV) This verse encourages courage and reminds us that God’s presence empowers us to face physical and emotional challenges.

L – Legacy (Ownership: Spirit, Worship, Prayer, Word)

Definition: The impact and inheritance we leave, rooted in spiritual values and accountability. Verse: “The righteous man walks in his integrity; his children are blessed after him.” — Proverbs 20:7 (NKJV) This verse reflects the lasting influence of a life lived with integrity, highlighting the blessings passed on through one’s spiritual legacy.

E – Endurance (Capital: Finances, Learning, Execution)

Definition: Putting plans into action with diligence and commitment to purpose. Verse: “Commit your works to the Lord, and your thoughts will be established.” — Proverbs 16:3 (NKJV) This verse emphasizes that dedication to God brings guidance and success, aligning our efforts with His wisdom as we carry out our plans.

F – Faithfulness (Kinship: Relationships)

Definition: Loyalty, commitment, and love that uphold and strengthen connections. Verse: “Let not mercy and truth forsake you; bind them around your neck, write them on the tablet of your heart.” — Proverbs 3:3 (NKJV) This verse encourages faithfulness in relationships, underscoring the importance of loyalty and love in building strong bonds.

T – Trust (with Intention: Building The Foundation of Bonds and Relationships)

Definition: Trusting God while setting clear and purposeful intentions, forming the foundation of meaningful relationships. Verse: “let us not love in word or in tongue, but in deed and in truth.” - 1 John 3:18(NKJV) This verse emphasizes intentionally demonstrating genuine love through meaningful actions.
